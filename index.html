How is weather in different cities impacted by their distance from the Equator?

The assignment uses technologies like Python, Pandas, MatplotLib, JSON etc. to analyze and answer.

I followed following approach to do my analysis and complete my assignment -

i. Assigned over 1200 random Latitude and Longitudes and used Python's CitiPy library to get list of closest cities.

ii. Used OpenWeatherMapAPI to get weather details for those cities.

iii.Created Dataframe of weather information of over 600 cities and generated a CSV file.

iv. This csv was used with MetplotLib scatter plots to generate following plots to show relationship Temperatre, Humidity, Cloudiness and Wind-speed with Latitude.

  • Temperature (F) vs. Latitude
  • Humidity (%) vs. Latitude
  • Cloudiness (%) vs. Latitude
  • Wind Speed (mph) vs. Latitude
